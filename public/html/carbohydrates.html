Carbohydrates

How carbs fit into a healthy diet

As part of healthy eating, everyone needs the same type of things, such as vitamins, minerals, carbohydrates, protein and fat. These are called nutrients. Together, nutrients in food help the body work.

Carbohydrates are part of a healthy diet. But some carb choices may be better for you than others...

What are carbohydrates?

Carbohydrates are sugars in food... naturally found in fruits, vegetables, nuts, seeds, grains and dairy products.

Types of carbohydrates

There are three main types of carbohydrates:

  • Simple carbohydrates
  • Complex carbohydrates
  • Fiber

Simple carbohydrates

Simple carbohydrates are sugars... making them a quick source of energy.

Complex carbohydrates

Complex carbohydrates are made up of long chains... a better source of energy.

Fiber

Fiber is a type of carbohydrate that the body cannot digest... support overall health.
